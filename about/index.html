About Cut the Rope Home

Cut the Rope Home started as a simple Discord server about modding the original Cut the Rope. Over time, we decided to change our scope, and, while our main focus is still modding, we wanted to give something else to the general community.

Who we are

Cut the Rope Home is made by aidrum and TheAwesomeBlue as a place to share stuff for the Cut the Rope community.

We are not affiliated with ZeptoLab or Cut the Rope in any way, shape or form. We simply enjoy the games they have made over the years.

Why we made the site

We made this website as a place to have everything a Cut the Rope fan could ever want! Music, app icons, game downloads, and more! We tried to find the optimal version of every game for every (major) platform (No ads, high quality graphics!) and found an extremely obscure version of Cut the Rope Holiday Gift in the process.
